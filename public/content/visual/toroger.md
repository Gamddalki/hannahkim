> Overview

To Roger (A5, 154p) is an independently written and self-published fiction set in a zombie apocalypse, focusing on how humanity persists in desolation. Through iterative editing, visual prototyping, and self-publication, I shaped a cohesive narrative and aesthetic that reflects both the fragility and strength of love.
