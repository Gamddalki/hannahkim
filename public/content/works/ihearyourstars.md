> Motivation

This project set out to explore how algorithmic composition can be combined with playful interaction to create and share personalized musical experiences. Inspired by the **pin cylinder** in music box which resembles starry sky, we aimed to transform user’s name, constellation, and favorite star into music — bridging data and emotion through a web-based music box.

> Approach

The goal was to make web-based interaction feel **tactile and intimate**, evoking the nostalgic act of winding a real music box. To achieve this, we conducted reference research to understand how the sensory experience of a physical music box could be reinterpreted in a web environment. Based on these insights, we focused on designing the music playback interface as the core of the project.

![Playback interface sketch](/img/projects/IHearYourStars2v1.png)

We also developed our **own rule-based algorithm for melody generation**, ensuring that each composition was uniquely created from user inputs rather than relying on AI. In addition, we built the data input page and the final sharing interface, allowing users to create, experience, and distribute their personalized music box seamlessly.

As the frontend developer, I engineered the interactive **playback mechanics** and developed the **rotating crank feature** that replicated the physical winding motion of a music box. The crank’s rotation speed directly influences the melody’s tempo, while I optimized visual–audio synchronization for a smooth, immersive experience.

> Outcomes

- Generated a personalized melody composition algorithm using user input data.
- Implemented a crank-based music box interaction with real-time speed-sensitive playback, showcasing frontend engineering that merged physical metaphors with digital interactivity.
- Developed a tokenized sharing system via unique URLs.

> Reflection

This project deepened my understanding of how **tangible metaphors** can enrich digital interactions. Designing the speed-sensitive playback challenged me to combine creative ideation with technical precision, ultimately strengthening my ability to build emotionally engaging, human-centered systems.
