> Motivation

The project began with a fascination for how ancient myths could be reimagined through gameplay mechanics rather than just text. Inspired by the **phonetic link between Sun the Light (Helios) and Thunderlight (Zeus)**, I built the concept around the conflict between the fading Titan Helios and the Olympian gods, exploring the mythic **power shift from the Titans to Zeus**. Within the constraints of a 3-day hackathon, I aimed to craft a fast-paced arcade game that carried narrative weight — reflecting themes of divine pride, loss, and rebellion directly through game interactions.

![Character Design Helios & Zeus](/img/projects/SunTheLight2.png)

> Approach

Our team adopted rapid prototyping to iterate quickly on narrative-driven gameplay.

- Narrative-Mechanic Integration

  I developed the myth-based storyline and integrated Helios's journey directly into the level progression. I also designed mechanics that mirrored the mythic stakes. For instance, flying too low burns villages, while flying too high provokes the constellations to cry.

![Gameplay Screen](/img/projects/SunTheLight3.png)

- Audiovisual Immersion

  I composed retro-style original soundtrack and created pixel-art cutscenes to visualize the mythic conflict. Key encounters, such as the sub-boss Apollo and the final confrontation with Zeus, vividly embodied the struggle between the fading Titans and rising Olympians. This theme was capped by unique ending animations dynamically determined by the player's survival stage, ensuring that the story and gameplay were inseparable.

> Outcomes

![Outcomes](/img/projects/SunTheLight4.png)

- **1st Place Winner** at the 3rd MaKINGJam (Ewha Womans University) for outstanding originality and thematic completeness.
- Successfully integrated a complex myth-based narrative into a cohesive arcade experience within a 3-day development cycle.
- Produced an **original soundtrack and visual assets** that enhanced emotional immersion, demonstrating a multidisciplinary approach to game creation.

> Reflection

Unlike previous projects where a gap between vision and technical skill caused friction, my strengthened proficiency in Unity and C# allowed me to seamlessly translate a complex narrative vision into a cohesive gameplay experience. This project taught me that **technical rigor is the foundation of creative freedom**, empowering me to lead the team not just in management, but in creative and technical integration.
