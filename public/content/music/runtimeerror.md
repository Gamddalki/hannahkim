> Overview

A runtime error refers to an error that occurs while a program is running — often caused by an infinite loop that causes a program to run forever. Likewise, stepping out of the comfort zone of everyday life and facing new challenges can make you nervous. “Runtime Error” is a song written to **encourage people to keep moving forward toward tomorrow, despite uncertainty**.

> Outcomes

*   Completed the original composition from songwriting and arrangement to live performance.
*   Premiered the piece as the headliner of a joint concert, receiving positive audience feedback for its concept and emotional delivery.
*   Recorded and mixed a live version from the concert and released it on SoundCloud.
