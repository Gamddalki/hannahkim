> Overview

“0505” is a song born from the desire to capture the fleeting flutter of youth. It is **our lifelong contract** left not on paper, but **in music** — a promise to stay together until spring flowers bloom in winter.

As the **Creative Director and Composer** for RGBN, I led the entire production lifecycle, from composing the full band score to directing the music video. My goal was to systematically engineer an emotional nostalgia through sound and visuals.

- Musical Composition

  I wrote the lyrics and arranged the full score for all instruments (drums, bass, two guitars, keys, vocals). To evoke the “fleeting wonder of youth,” I deliberately designed the soundscape with retro synth textures, distortions, and rhythmic variations.

- Visual Narrative

  I directed, filmed, and edited the music video to extend the song’s story. Using two different aspect ratios and color grading, I visualized the protagonist’s perspective of finding a camera on the street and reliving cherished memories through its viewfinder. This created a seamless synchronization between the auditory build-up and the visual storytelling.

> Outcomes

- Composed and released RGBN’s first original single on SoundCloud, managing the entire recording and production process.
- Directed and produced a narrative music video for YouTube, successfully translating the song’s auditory themes into a visual narrative.
- Received high praise from renowned poet **Prof. Jeong Kkeut-byeol** for the lyrical depth and narrative structure, recognizing the work as a successful fusion of literary sensibility and multimedia production.
